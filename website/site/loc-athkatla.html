Athkatla

Type: City
Region: Amn
Nickname: City of Coin

The "City of Coin" — Toz's home and the seat of the Greenbottle family, a well-known mercantile clan with a fleet of sailing vessels. Toz captained their newest commission, the True Hand, on his first command.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
