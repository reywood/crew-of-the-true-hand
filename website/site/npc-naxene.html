Naxene

Mentioned in sessions: 2026-03-08 2026-05-12 2026-06-16
Type: Ally
Location: Waterdeep
First Seen: 2026-03-08

A noble of Waterdeep first met at the Golden Fields inn — stand-offish at first, ink-stained fingers, deep in her own notes on widespread giant activity. Hal earned her trust. She gave the crew a letter for Chazlauth in Waterdeep and later put them up at her guest house. She believes good dragons may be part of the answer.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
