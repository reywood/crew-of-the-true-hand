Goliath of Cormeer

Mentioned in sessions: 2026-06-16
Based at: Waterdeep
Type: Acquaintance
Location: Waterdeep
Race: Goliath
First Seen: 2026-06-16

An unnamed goliath the crew met being turned away at Waterdeep's south gate, traveling with his wife. A guard mistook him for a giant and tried to bar entry; Fiz recognized him as a goliath — giant-kin, not a giant — and vouched for him, and the couple was let through. He said he was on his way to work for the ship-dealer Brindle, and swore he would remember the kindness.

His personal name is never given. "Cormeer" — heard variously as Cormir / Kermir / Cormyr — is his homeland (a far-southern nation), not his name; earlier notes mistook the place for the man.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
