Holy book of Valkur

Mentioned in sessions: 2026-06-16
Type: Book
Holder: Toz
Status: Active
Origin: 2026-06-16

The scripture of Valkur, god of sailors, given to Toz by the priest at the Wave Hall in Waterdeep after Toz confessed his new fear of water and the malevolent presence he has been dreaming of. Toz left with the book and a set of penance-tasks to attempt.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
