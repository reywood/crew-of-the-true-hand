The True Hand

Mentioned in sessions: 2025-09-23 2025-11-12 2026-01-27 2026-02-10 2026-06-16
Governed by: Toz
Type: Ship (wrecked)
Captain: Toz
Home Port: Athkatla

Toz's first command — a Greenbottle-financed ship that met an untimely end just eleven months after her maiden voyage, wrecked off the coast near Nightstone. Four crew survived: Ol' Rourke (navigator), Fish Hook Finn (deck hand), Liera (cook, of the Pearl Isles), and Garret "Ox" Dorn (sailor). From the wreckage the crew salvaged an ornate bottle inscribed in Primordial: "To speak my name is to unmake chains."

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
