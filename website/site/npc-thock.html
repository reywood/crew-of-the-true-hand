Thock

Type: Enemy (slain)
Location: Nightstone goblin cave
First Seen: 2025-11-12
Race: Ogre

The ogre that led the raid on Nightstone from the goblin cave near Ardeep Forest. Fiz and Woz drew him out of the cave and killed him. His ogre wife came out next and met the same end.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
