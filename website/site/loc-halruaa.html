Halruaa

Mentioned in sessions: 2026-01-27 2026-06-16
Type: Nation
Region: South
Government: Technocracy

The southern technocracy Fiz hails from. Leaving Halruaa to see the world is frowned upon by its society; Fiz did it anyway, in a stolen flying ship that crashed just outside the border. He has at least one old Harper friend from his Halruaa days.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
