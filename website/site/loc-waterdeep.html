The City of Splendors. The crew arrived to find their faces on the cover of the Waterdeep Wazoo. Here they met Chazlauth (and his illegal silver dragon), Vandal Lovelace at the Harshnag statue, and Garley — whose runaway mechanical statue Fiz and Toz saved a crowd from. They also reunited with Ol' Rourke at the Deep Water Inn on the docks, where a small crowd was abruptly mind-controlled into attacking the room. The Yawning Portal — most popular tavern in the city — is still on the to-visit list.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
