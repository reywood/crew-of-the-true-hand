Spine of the World

Mentioned in sessions: 2026-03-08
Type: Mountain range
Region: Far North

A vast mountain range in the far North. The Oracle is said to dwell here; reaching her requires the giant Harshnag as guide and a giant conch as a key.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
