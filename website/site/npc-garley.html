Garley Gond

Type: Ally
Location: Waterdeep
Role: Inventor
First Seen: 2026-06-16

Built a two-story walking mechanical statue in Waterdeep. Something went wrong with it mid-stride on a public street; Fiz and Toz cushioned the fall with Feather Fall, and Fiz diagnosed the fault. Out of gratitude, Garley gave Fiz a magical alchemy jug taken from the statue's belly.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
