Brindle Stormtide

A halfling ship-seller who works the Waterdeep docks. Ol' Rourke believes Brindle can set the crew up with a new ship, and Cormeer the goliath was on his way to work for him when the crew met the couple at the city gate. Two independent leads, one Brindle.
