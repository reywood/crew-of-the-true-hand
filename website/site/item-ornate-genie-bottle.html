Ornate genie bottle

Mentioned in sessions: 2025-09-23 2025-12-07 2026-01-13
Who could help: Derothra Shendrel, Molak
Type: Mystery
Holder: Party
Status: Unresolved
Origin: 2025-09-23
Expertise Needed: genies, Primordial

Salvaged from the wreck of the True Hand on the crew's first steps ashore. Etched in Primordial: "To speak my name is to unmake chains." Molak, the retired-adventurer innkeeper who commissioned its recovery, admitted he had meant to keep it and take the wish for himself; instead he pressed it back on the crew and named Derothra Shendrel of Triboar as the one who knows the genie's true name. Speak the name, Molak said, and the bottle grants a wish.
