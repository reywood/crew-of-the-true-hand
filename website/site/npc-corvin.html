Corvin

Mentioned in sessions: 2025-12-17 2026-01-13
Type: Politically uneasy
Location: Nightstone
First Seen: 2025-12-17

Claimant who took control of Nightstone after Lady Nandor's death in the cloud giant attack. He carries royal blood and is technically within his rights to rule, but neither Daphne nor Xolkin trusts him — and he seized Aganor's house among others. Whether his rule benefits Nightstone is unresolved.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
