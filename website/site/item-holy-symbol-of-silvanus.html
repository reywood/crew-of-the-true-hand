Holy symbol of Silvanus

Mentioned in sessions: 2025-12-07
Type: Focus
Holder: Woz
Status: Active
Origin: 2025-12-07

A wooden holy symbol of Silvanus, the woodland god, inlaid with gold. Recovered from Boz Hark's stash in the Nightstone goblin cave and claimed by Woz — an odd fit for a cleric of Eldath, but the two greenwood powers are close enough that the workmanship still calls to him.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
