Boz Hark

Mentioned in sessions: 2025-12-07
Type: Enemy (slain)
Location: Nightstone goblin cave
First Seen: 2025-12-07
Race: Goblin

Boss of the goblin cave in Ardeep Forest, wearing a horned black hat and holding a captive Nightstone woman under one hand and a javelin in the other. Kept a pack of dog-sized rats he called his "babies" as personal guard. Wanted three gold a head for the Nightstone villagers and tried to trade their freedom for the crew dealing with a "little slime" down one of his tunnels — a deal he never meant to honour, per Snigbat, one of his own who defected. The crew killed him in the running fight at the cave mouth after the slime turned out to be beyond them.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
