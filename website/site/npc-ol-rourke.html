Ol' Rourke

Mentioned in sessions: 2025-09-23 2026-06-16
Based at: Waterdeep
Type: Old shipmate
Location: Waterdeep (docks)
Role: Navigator
First Seen: 2025-09-23

Navigator of the wrecked True Hand and one of the four survivors of the wreck near Nightstone. Now working the Waterdeep docks. The crew found him telling tales of the giant attack on the ship to a crowd at the Deep Water Inn — right before that crowd's eyes turned white and they were mind-controlled into attacking the room. Rourke told the crew that Brindle may be able to set them up with a new ship.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
