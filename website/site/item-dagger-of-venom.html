Dagger of Venom

Mentioned in sessions: 2026-02-10
Type: Weapon
Holder: Toz
Status: Active
Origin: 2026-02-10

A weeping dagger taken off the half-dragon rider at Goldenfields. Once per day, on a hit the wielder can force the target to save against poison or take extra poison damage and be poisoned for a minute.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
