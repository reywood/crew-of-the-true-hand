Chazlauth

Type: Ally
Location: Waterdeep
First Seen: 2026-06-16

A Waterdeep contact of Naxene's and an enthusiastic dragon collector — he keeps a small silver dragon, which is illegal, and seems entirely unbothered by that fact. He pointed the crew at the green dragon Old Gnawbone in Kryptgarden Forest as a possible giant-killing ally, mentioned the copper dragon Vexalanthus who patrols the High Road, and gave each of the crew a potion of poison resistance.

Tales from the sea and the giant-haunted North.
