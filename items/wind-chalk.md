A slim stick of chalk that drifts on its own across a laid-out map, scribbling lines and fading them again — mapping the wind. A gift from Zephyros, given after Toz told the tale of the *True Hand*'s wreck. Laid on a map, it grants advantage on navigation checks tied to wind and weather.
