A living circlet of vine pressed onto Fiz by Daniel Windraven at the Nightstone cave mouth — a Watch-token of the Emerald Enclave, the druidic network watching the sudden movements of giants across Faerûn. It withers the day Fiz turns enemy to the Enclave, and fades naturally after about an hour on its own besides.
