A sorcerer's focus taken from the half-dragon rider who fell at Goldenfields. Grants +1 to spell attack rolls, and on a short rest restores expended sorcery points. Its very existence — carried by a lone half-dragon with "his own aims" — is a small clue in itself about who sends such riders.
