A magical bladder recovered from the belly of the failing walking mechanical statue in Waterdeep. Its maker Garley Gond pressed it into Fiz's hands as thanks after Fiz diagnosed the fault. Fiz's tinker's eye recognised it at once as a proper alchemy jug — able to produce a range of liquids on command each day (fresh water, wine, oil, acid, and rarer besides).
