A sheaf of tightly-written notes, all in Draconic. Taken from the Bag of Holding of the kobold who died alongside a young blue dragon and a half-dragon rider at Goldenfields. No one aboard the crew reads Draconic. The two Scrolls of Tongues from the same bag might crack them open temporarily, or Chazlauth (Waterdeep's dragon-mad wizard) is the obvious first ask. Yackerty the Harper may know Draconic as well.
