# Quests

Active threads, dormant leads, and resolved jobs. Source session in parens.

## Main arc — the giant ordning

Giants of every kind are on the move and attacking settlements across the North. The party is trying to find the Oracle to understand why.

- **Reach the Oracle.** Path is Spine of the World, with the giant **Harshnag** as guide. Need a **giant conch** to call/contact the Oracle; a dragon may be the way to get it. (2026-03-08, 2026-06-16)
- **Find Harshnag.** Last known to be in Silverymoon. Was a member of the now-defunct adventuring party **Force Gray** out of Waterdeep. Statue of him stands in Waterdeep. (2026-03-08, 2026-06-16)
- **Get to Silverymoon via the Harpers.** Harpers have a secret teleportation portal network. Contact **Yackerty**, a dwarf Harper in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep. Fiz has an old Harper friend from Halruaa as a backup contact. (2026-06-16)
- **Visit the Eye of Annam.** The giant gods want to help but the party must come to them. (2026-02-10)
