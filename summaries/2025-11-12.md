*In brief: Cloud giants hit Nightstone for its namesake stone, and the crew tracks the kidnapped villagers to a goblin cave at the edge of Ardeep Forest.*

## A night at the inn

The crew slept at the Nightstone inn — or what survived of it. Most of the townsfolk had already fled into the forest. In the small hours, Xolkin's flying snake nosed in through their window before they shooed it back into its master's care. By morning a fresh fight came to them: a band of orcs that the party cut down on the outskirts of the village.

## The truth of the raid

Setting out to find Molak and deliver the djinn-bottle, the crew watched soldiers trying to flee Nightstone's keep — soldiers who took one look at the party and bolted back inside. Talking down the keep's guards, the crew got the story: cloud giants had dropped stones from the sky on Nightstone, and stolen the great black nightstone for which the village is named. **Lady Nandor** lay dead in the rubble, crushed by one of the falling stones.

A side thread surfaced in the talk: a bounty on Kella out of Darkhope, whose father is famous and lives in Darkar with her grandfather Teldor.

## Following the trail

The guards rode out with the crew to track the missing townsfolk. Footprints and signs of a scuffle led to a cave on the edge of Ardeep Forest.

Inside the cave's mouth, an Ardeep elf was mid-ritual, floating stones suspended around him. The crew helped him finish the rite. Looking deep into the cave they sensed a large stalactite humming with an evil presence, and learned that the goblins of this cave were the ones who had taken the Nightstone villagers.

The elf introduced himself: **Daniel Windraven** of the **Emerald Enclave**, a network of druidic circles that connects across the realms. He gave Fiz a vine bracelet as a token of friendship, and warned that giants have been stealing artifacts across Faerûn.

## Drawing out Thock

Fiz and Woz crept further into the cave and spotted a force of goblins led by an ogre named **Thock**. The two drew Thock out alone and dropped him. His ogre wife came charging out next — and met the same end.

With the ogres dead, the crew explored deeper. An elderly goblin woman, eager to switch sides, told them where the villagers were being held: a room full of bats. The crew got the villagers out alive.

## What's next

- The stalactite still hums in the dark. Something evil is inside it.
- The boss goblin and the rest of the cave's force remain alive.
- Daniel Windraven and the Emerald Enclave are now a friendly contact.
- Cloud giants took the nightstone. To what end?
- The Kella bounty remains open.
