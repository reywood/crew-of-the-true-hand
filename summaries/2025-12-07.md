*In brief: A truce with the boss goblin, a slime in a stalactite that the crew wisely walks away from, and a name in Triboar that could unchain the genie.*

## Striking a deal

With Thock dead and his goblins shaken, the crew negotiated with the boss goblin of the cave. The children of Nightstone were released into the care of a tiefling woman from the cave and led to safety. As part of the agreement, a goblin named **Snigbat** tagged along with the party, ostensibly to help deal with the slime still oozing in the stalactite.

## The slime in the stalactite

The crew did find the stalactite at the heart of the cave — and confirmed it housed an ooze of some kind. Looking it over, they weighed the fight, decided it was not theirs to pick today, and bailed.

## A fight for the cave

The retreat ran straight into the rest of the cave's goblins, who had little interest in a truce after their boss had been killed. The crew fought through, won, and looted some treasure. Among the spoils: Molak agreed they could keep the ornate genie bottle pulled from the wreck of the True Hand.

## The name in Triboar

Molak also gave them a lead. The genie's true name — the word that would "unmake chains" per the bottle's Primordial inscription — is known to **Derothra Shendrel** in Triboar.

The session closed with the party reaching level four.

## What's next

- Travel south to Triboar to find Derothra Shendrel.
- The slime in the stalactite remains, biding its time.
