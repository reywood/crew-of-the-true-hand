A city in the Silver Marches. Harshnag is believed to be in or near Silverymoon, and Baros Ravenkind here can craft a silver eagle messenger to reach Zephyros. The crew's intended route is via the Harpers' secret portal network, contacted through Yackerty in Waterdeep.
