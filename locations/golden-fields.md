A farming town about twice the size of Nightstone, anchored by an abbey to Shontea — a wooden statue of the plump, smiling goddess surrounded by wheat stands at its heart. The town has been suffering hill giant raids: bugbears and goblins sneak in to steal food while giants pelt the walls with stones. The crew defended the town through a follow-up raid and learned, by insight, that the hill giants are simply gathering food for their queen, Guh.
