Innkeeper of Nightstone and the local gossip hub — Molak knows what is stirring in the area better than anyone. He is himself a retired adventurer, and the crew hired on to deliver a salvaged ornate bottle to him from the wreck of the *True Hand*. When they finally sat down with him in the cleared goblin cave, he admitted he had meant to keep the bottle and take up its wish himself, but pressed it back on them along with the name he had been holding: **Derothra Shendrel**, leader of Triboar, who is said to know the genie's true name. In parting he mentioned that hill giants had been raiding silos down in the Golden Fields.
