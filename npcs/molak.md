Innkeeper of Nightstone and the local gossip hub — Molak knows what is stirring in the area better than anyone. The crew first met him to deliver a salvaged ornate bottle pulled from the wreck of the True Hand. After dealing with the goblin cave and the slime in the stalactite, he let them keep the bottle. He also pointed them toward Derothra Shendrel in Triboar, who is said to know the genie's name.
